                       
                   OSprober Toggle
                        
    OSprober is a utility to add GRUB boot entries
    for every operating system currently installed
    on your computer.   
    
    Use the toggle button to switch OSprober on or 
    off. The log is in /var/log/grub-update.log
    
    Please wait for grub to finish updating before 
    rebooting. Look for the progress update dialog
    to disappear upon completion.
    
    Please Note - You must be booted into the main
    partition controlling grub. Generally, this is
    the last distribution installed.
